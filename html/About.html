History of KMJ Recording Studio

KMJ Recording Studio was opened in 2019.It is owned and managed by Kareem Paul. It was created to be an inspiration to musicians all around the world regardless of the genre, ethnicity, language or economic status. It is a space where you could come and feel free to express your creativity through music. It has seen the likes of Nyol Manswell and up-and-coming artists such as Nikeisha Pierre and Wayne Smith.

The Big Idea Behind KMJRecording Studio

  • 1. Promotes the Studio’s Services.
  • 2. Allows users to explore the content through visual and audio descriptions.
  • 3. Showcases Work already done at this studio.
  • 4. Features an accessible portfolio with audio samples and detailed descriptions so all users can evaluate the studio’s capabilities.
  • 5. Enables Easy Booking.
